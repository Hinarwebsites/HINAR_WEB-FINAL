// Tentang Kami · About Us

Built on Indonesian Military Tradition

HINAR (Headquarters of the Indonesian National Army) is Roblox's premier Indonesian military roleplay group. Modelled on the real TNI AD (Tentara Nasional Indonesia Angkatan Darat), we operate with authentic ranks, multiple divisions, and a structured chain of command spanning from recruit to Panglima Tertinggi.

We conduct scheduled training operations run by KODIKLAT instructors, joint exercises between all active divisions, and large-scale base defence operations under KOSTRAD and KOPASSUS leadership. Every member earns their rank through real performance — not time served.

HINAR is more than a game group — it is a community built on Indonesian military tradition, mutual respect, and continuous improvement. With 71+ members and growing, multiple active divisions, and the TNI SIM Project in development, the New Era has only just begun.

Whether you are a new recruit starting Basic Training or a veteran soldier aiming for officer command, there is a clear path forward. Your discipline determines your destiny.

// Pertanyaan Umum · Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Q: How do I join HINAR?

Join the Roblox group and the Discord server, read all the rules, then attend the next Basic Training session run by KODIKLAT. After graduating you receive your first rank and can request a division assignment.

Q: How do I get promoted?

Promotions are never given on request. They are earned through consistent activity, discipline, respect for the chain of command, and strong performance in training and operations. Your divisional commander will promote you when they judge you are ready.

Q: Can I be in more than one division?

Generally no — each member is assigned to one primary division. However, exceptional members may be seconded to KOPASSUS or special operations on a temporary basis by command decision.

Q: How do I join KOPASSUS?

KOPASSUS is invitation only. You cannot apply. Commanders observe members across all divisions and extend invitations to those who consistently demonstrate exceptional skill, discipline, and loyalty. Focus on being the best soldier in your current division.

Q: What happens if I break the rules?

Depending on severity: a Warning, additional training punishment, rank demotion, or removal from the community (kick/ban). The Military Police (POMAD) handles investigations and reports violations to senior command for action.

Q: I've been inactive — will I lose my rank?

Extended inactivity without notice may result in a rank review. If you know you'll be away, inform your divisional commander in advance. Active members who return after leave in good standing are generally welcomed back.

Q: What is the TNI SIM Project?

It is HINAR's upcoming full simulation experience on Roblox — authentic bases, real-time joint operations, vehicle systems, and rank integration. It is currently in development (Phase 1). Follow the Discord for updates.

Q: How do I report a player for misconduct?

Report via the Discord server to the Military Police (POMAD) or a senior moderator. Include the offender's username, what happened, when it happened, and any evidence (screenshots/video). Do not take matters into your own hands.

// Latihan Dasar · Basic Training

Phase 1 — Basic Training (KODIKLAT)

01

Arrival & Briefing

Report to the designated training base. Instructors will brief you on rules, conduct, and what to expect. Listen carefully — interrupting an instructor is a violation.

02

Formation Drills

Learn standard military formations — line, column, and wedge. You will be tested on moving in formation under instructor command without breaking rank.

03

Weapons Handling

Introduction to approved weapons. Covers safe handling, when you may and may not draw your weapon, and basic combat stances. Random weapon discharge = automatic fail.

04

Conduct & Communication

How to address superiors, how to request permission to speak, proper in-game and Discord communication etiquette. Military formality is expected at all times during training.

✓

Graduation

Pass all phases and receive your first rank — Prajurit Dua. You are now an official soldier of HINAR and may request assignment to a division.

// Sistem Permainan · Game & Server Systems

Game Systems

HINAR's Roblox experience runs on a set of in-game systems that keep the server fair, organized, and immersive. Here's how each one works.

// Sistem Kenaikan Pangkat · Panel Promotion

🎖 Panel Promotion System

The Panel Promotion is an in-game admin tool used exclusively by ranked command staff (NCO and above) to manage member ranks directly inside the Roblox server. It replaces manual, error-prone rank tracking with a controlled, logged system.

🔐

Restricted Access

Only command staff with the correct permission level can open the panel. Regular members cannot promote themselves or others.

📋

Logged Actions

Every promotion or demotion through the panel is logged with who issued it, the rank change, and the timestamp — visible to senior command for accountability.

⚙️

Synced In-Game

Rank changes apply instantly to your in-game tag, rank board, and uniform access — no manual group-rank syncing needed.

🚫

No Self-Requests

The panel is for command use only. Asking a panel-holder to promote you is treated the same as requesting a promotion — it counts against you.

// Sistem Anti-Cheat · Anti-Cheat System

🛡 Anti-Cheat System

HINAR's server runs an automated anti-cheat layer alongside active Military Police moderation to protect the integrity of training and operations. It is designed to detect and stop unfair play before it disrupts the experience for everyone else.

⚡

Speed & Movement Checks

Flags abnormal walk/run speed, flight, noclip, and teleport-style exploits in real time.

🎯

Weapon Integrity

Detects impossible fire rates, aimbot-style snapping, and unauthorized weapon/tool injection.

📝

Auto-Logging

Suspicious activity is automatically logged and reported to Military Police for manual review — false positives are appealable.

⛔

Auto-Kick / Ban

Confirmed exploiting results in an automatic kick; repeat or severe offences escalate to a permanent ban from the server.

Zero Tolerance: Cheating, exploiting, or using third-party injection software is a permanent-ban offence regardless of rank. Per Rule 04, this includes "cheating / exploiting" as a strictly prohibited action.

// Pertahanan Udara · AA — Anti-Air Defense

🚀 AA (Anti-Air) Defense System

AA Defense refers to HINAR's anti-air emplacements and protocols used during base defence operations. As the Air Forces (Angkatan Udara) division comes online, ground units rely on AA systems to deny enemy air superiority over friendly territory.

🏗

Static Emplacements

Fixed AA turrets are positioned around bases during defence operations, manned by KOSTRAD or assigned AA crew.

📡

Airspace Denial

AA crews track and engage hostile aircraft to keep enemy air support away from ground troops during an operation.

👥

Crew Assignment

AA posts are assigned by the operation commander before the session starts — never claimed without orders.

🤝

Joint Coordination

AA operators coordinate with ground commanders over voice/Discord to call out enemy approach vectors and hold fire on friendlies.

Note: full AA mechanics will expand alongside the Angkatan Udara (Air Forces) division and the TNI SIM Project rollout.

Performance-Based

Demotion Path
